ABOUT THE PROJECT

SEMESTER OUTLOOK - A WEBPAGE

The webpage gets students test experience and helps the teacher to analyzes the students in all possible ways and make semester exams tranquil . The website is developed using HTML, CSS, PHP and database support is assited by mysql. The required results can be exported using SQL queries , and can be analyzed and marked . According to the college curriculum the college has 3 classtests and each of the cts are evaluated and analyzed by the teachers.The ultimate result of the project is to help the students score good marks, by making the teachers analyze the students experience and take action like [ Reteaching , extra classes , model or pre semesters ] before semesters .
